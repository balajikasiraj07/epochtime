Daylight Saving Time (DST)

Timezones that observe DST shift by 1 hour in summer:

  • PST → PDT (UTC−7): 2nd Sunday March → 1st Sunday November
  • EST → EDT (UTC−4): same schedule as PST/PDT
  • GMT → BST (UTC+1): last Sunday March → last Sunday October
  • IST, JST, CST: no DST — fixed year-round
