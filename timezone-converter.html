Daylight saving time (DST)

Many timezones observe DST and shift by 1 hour during summer. Key examples:

  • PST → PDT (UTC−7): second Sunday in March to first Sunday in November
  • EST → EDT (UTC−4): same schedule as PST/PDT
  • GMT → BST (UTC+1): last Sunday in March to last Sunday in October
  • CET → CEST (UTC+2): same as GMT/BST
  • IST, JST, CST: no DST — offset is fixed year-round
