How to calculate days between two dates

Simply pick a start date and end date above. The calculator instantly shows the exact difference in days, weeks, months, years, hours, minutes, seconds, and business days. Use the Today button to anchor either date to the current day.

Business days vs calendar days

Calendar days count every day including weekends and holidays. Business days (also called working days) count only Monday–Friday, excluding Saturdays and Sundays. The calculator shows both so you can use whichever is relevant — calendar days for general use, business days for project timelines, contracts, and SLA calculations.
